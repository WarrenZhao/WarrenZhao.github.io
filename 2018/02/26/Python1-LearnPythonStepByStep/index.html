Comments

Comments are any text to the right of the # symbol and is mainly useful as notes for the reader of the program. the # symbol can both in the right of the codes and in a new line. Comments should tell me why I do this. This is useful for readers of your program so that they can easily understand what the program is doing. Remember, that person can be yourself after six months!

Use as many useful comments as you can in your program to:

  • explain assumptions
  • explain important decisions
  • explain important details
  • explain problem you’re trying to solve
  • explain problems you’re trying to overcom in your program, etc.

Quotes

  • There are three types of quote in python - single quote, double quotes and triple quote.
  • Stirngs in single Quote work exactly the same way as strings in single quotes.
  • You can specify multi-line strings using triple quotes. You can use single quotes and double quotes freely within the triple quotes.
  • Once you have created a string, you cannot change it.
